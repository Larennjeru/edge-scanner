How It Works

The Edge Scanner doesn't guess. It runs 47 pairs through a 5-stage intelligence pipeline and only fires when everything aligns.

Market Data

Live OHLCV candles fetched from massive.com for all 47 pairs across M15, H1, H4 and D1 timeframes simultaneously.

2
MTF Confluence

Weighted confluence scoring across 4 timeframes. Daily chart is the veto — if D1 disagrees, the signal dies. No exceptions.

Real-time news from Reuters, Bloomberg, WSJ analysed with NLP. Central bank language. Fed tone. Macro catalysts. All scored.

7 models vote: XGBoost, LightGBM, Random Forest, MLP, ExtraTrees, GBM, Logistic. Trained on your real closed trades. Gets sharper daily.
